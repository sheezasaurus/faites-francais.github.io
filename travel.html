AU GUICHET

At the ticket office

Cultural tip Most train stations have automatic ticket machines. Their job is to accept credit and debit cards alongside cash. How convenient!

When it comes to travelling, France is ideal for its variety in places to see. Have a look at the interactive map below, with places that are worth visiting. Whether you are in the mood to sit by the sea, or dine with the Eiffel Tower as scenery, France is generous, with her landscapes that are jaw-dropping and eye-catching.

ALLER ET PRENDRE

To go and to take

Say these different forms of aller out loud. This will be useful in telling others where you are going.

Aller: to go
je vais (juh vay)) I go
tu vas (tew vah) you go (informal singular)
il/ elle va (eel/ el vah) he/ she goes
nous allons (noo zalloñ) we go
vous allez (voo zallay) you go (formal singular or plural)
ils/ elles vont (eel/ el voñ) they go

Say these different forms of prendre out loud. This will be useful in telling others that you take different modes of transport.

Prendre: to take
je prends (juh proñ) I take
tu prends (tew proñ) you take (informal singular)
il/ elle prend (eel/ el proñ) he/ she takes
nous prenons (noo prunoñ) we take
vous prenez (voo prunay) you take (formal singular or plural)
ils/ elles prennent (eel/ el pren) they take
