About

Bienvenue!

Welcome to Faites Français, a website dedicated to French language learning. Faites Français contains several resources created to develop your understanding of French:

  • Listen to audio recordings of everyday words and phrases
  • Learn about France, her culture and the history of the language in the country
  • Match words to pictures

    • I wish you the best! Plenty of hints will be provided with every exercise and activity. This website is more than a phrasebook. With cultural tips, pronunciation advice and audio, you will feel enriched, being able to travel to France more independently.
