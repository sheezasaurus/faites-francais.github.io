Sightseeing

Cultural tip Most public buildings and private offices in France are shut durng public holidays. You will find most offices to be closed throughout August. In fact, if a holiday falls on a Thursday, the French tend to take the following Friday as a holiday, also! This is the French way to create a long weekend...
