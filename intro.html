BONJOUR

Hello

In France, it is customary to greet loved ones and acquaintances with kisses on the cheek. There is no specific number of kisses, as the amount can vary from two to four. For example, three kisses is normal in the south but in Brittany, two kisses are considered to be the norm. However, should there be a more formal situation- such as a meeting- a handshake will suffice!
